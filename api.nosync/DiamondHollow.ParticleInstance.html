Class ParticleInstance

Will appear as a collection of small colored rectangles dispersed around a point. They will move around and disappear after a certain amount of time. They may or may not collide with platforms.

Constructors

ParticleInstance(DiamondHollowGame, ParticleController, ParticleConstructor)

Creates a new particle instance.

The particles' colors will be randomly selected from the provided texture. If not specified, the color will be a single color.

The particles will have randomized positions, velocities, and lifetimes. See ParticleConstructor for more information.

Do not use this constructor directly. Use Spawn(ParticleConstructor) instead.
